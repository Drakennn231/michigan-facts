Interesting Facts About Michigan

Famous for Its Lakes

Michigan borders four of the five Great Lakes and has more freshwater shoreline than any other state.

State Symbols

  • State Bird: American Robin
  • State Flower: Apple Blossom
  • State Tree: Eastern White Pine

Other Interesting Things

Detroit is known as the birthplace of the automotive industry. The Mackinac Bridge is one of the longest suspension bridges in the world.
